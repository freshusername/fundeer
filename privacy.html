Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 09.02.2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Fundeer (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use our App.

2. Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or provide any personally identifiable information. All data used by the App (for example, financial entries or user preferences) is stored locally on your device using Swift Data. We do not collect or store any personal data on external servers, nor do we track or log your usage patterns.

3. How We Use Your Information

Because all data is kept locally, we do not access or process any personal information. The App’s functionalities work entirely on-device. Any data you enter (like budgeting entries or financial goals) remains solely on your device and is used only to provide the App's features (e.g., calculating totals or generating summaries).

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not share, sell, rent, or otherwise disclose any data to third parties. No user data is transmitted to external services or analytics providers.

5. Data Retention & Security

Because we store data locally on your device, retention depends on whether you keep the App installed. Once uninstalled, all locally stored data is deleted. We employ standard iOS security practices to safeguard your information on-device; however, please ensure you protect your device with a passcode or other secure methods.

6. Your Rights

You may delete or modify your data at any time by changing or removing entries within the App. Since personal information is not collected, there is no separate online portal or customer service channel required for managing personal data.

7. Children’s Privacy

This App is suitable for children under the age of 13 as it does not collect personal data. We comply with applicable children’s privacy laws, including the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), by not knowingly collecting information from children. If you believe we may have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with a revised Effective Date, and continued use of the App after such changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
